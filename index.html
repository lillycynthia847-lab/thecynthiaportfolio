The Girl in the Paddock

Hi, I'm Cynthia Wanjira — a Formula 1 content creator who brings editorial flair to the fast-paced world of motorsport. When I'm not trackside capturing the adrenaline, I'm crafting UGC that makes brands feel as thrilling as a last-lap overtake.
